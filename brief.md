# Megamall Online Store UI/UX Redesign Brief

## 1. Company Chosen

I chose Megamall Online Store, an ecommerce company that sells consumer products online. Their current website is: https://www.megamallonline.store

## 2. How I Found Them

I found Megamall Online Store through a Google search while researching ecommerce businesses that could benefit from a stronger and more modern online shopping experience.

## 3. Why I Chose This Company

I chose this company because it already has a clear online retail business model, but the website experience does not feel as polished, trustworthy, or user-friendly as modern ecommerce standards. I saw a strong opportunity to improve the way customers browse products, understand the brand, and feel confident about purchasing.

## 4. Why the Website Needs a Redesign

The current website needs a redesign because several issues make the experience feel less effective and less trustworthy for customers:

- The visual design feels crowded and inconsistent, with too many elements competing for attention at once.
- The mobile experience appears weak, with cramped layouts and less comfortable navigation on smaller screens.
- The navigation and product discovery flow could be clearer, which makes it harder for users to move quickly through the site.
- Calls-to-action are not always prominent enough, which can reduce confidence and conversion.
- The overall interface feels less polished than expected for a modern ecommerce store, especially when compared with current best practices in online retail.
- Accessibility and usability issues may also be present because the experience does not consistently support clear hierarchy, strong spacing, readable content, and easier interaction on mobile.
- One of the most important trust issues I noticed is that customers may struggle to find location and contact details at the bottom of the page because the content keeps loading and shifting the experience. This can interrupt the user and make it harder to quickly confirm business legitimacy, physical address, and support information.
- The lack of a clear, stable, and easy-to-find contact and location section can affect customer trust, especially for an ecommerce business where shoppers want reassurance before buying.

I reviewed the current website visually during the audit process, and these observations were used as the main basis for the redesign direction. The goal of the redesign is to make the experience cleaner, more premium, easier to use on mobile, and more trustworthy for shoppers.

## Footer

Support information relocated into a dedicated footer containing contact details, business hours, FAQs, delivery information, returns, payment methods, newsletter signup, and social links, allowing users to stay focused on shopping while still having quick access to support.
